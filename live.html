All
TeenHacks LI and NYIT are not responsible for any lost or damaged property. Please keep a eye on all your belongings throughout the event.

All participants are required to wear the TeenHacks LI badge at all times. If you see anyone without a badge or guest pass wandering the premises, please alert an organizer/NYIT staff.

TeenHacks LI is abiding by the MLH Code of Conduct. In short, be respectful. Harassment and abuse are never tolerated. If you feel uncomfortable at any point, please approach a TeenHacks LI Organizer immediately.

Devpost

Use Devpost to submit your project for judging. If you want to be assigned a judge, you must make a Devpost submission by Sunday, 1pm. If you need help, please let an organizer know before the submission deadline.
